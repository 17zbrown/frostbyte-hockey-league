Game Reports

Stats import automatically from EA
After each scheduled game, your full box score — goals, assists, shots, hits, PIM and goalie saves — is pulled in automatically from EA and posted here, usually within a few minutes on game nights. You don't need to submit anything. If a game ever doesn't import (played off-schedule, or a club isn't linked to its EA club yet), send your EA end-of-game screenshots to the commissioners and they'll add it.
